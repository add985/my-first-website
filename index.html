BATA-PAKISTAN'S FAVOURITE FOOTWEAR BRAND

Since 1942 Bata Pakistan has been rendering its services to its valued customers by offering quality products. It was incorporated in Pakistan as Bata Shoe Company (Pakistan) Limited in 1951 and went public to become Bata Pakistan Limited in the year 1979 Since its inception, the company has not only maintained a good reputation of manufacturing high quality footwear for all segments but has also been designing shoes in accordance with the changing fashions and trends.
